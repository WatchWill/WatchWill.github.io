Welcome to the Tetocord TF2 Server!

Welcome to the Tetocord TF2 server! We hope you enjoy your stay! As this server is operated by the same team as the Kasane Teto Discord server, all the same rules apply here, namely:

Server Rules

  1. Be Respectful! Trash talk is allowed, so long as it stays within reasonable limits. Do not disrepect one another.
  2. No Bigotry! Homophobic, transphobic and ableist language is not permitted in the server, and are subject to swift, harsh punishments.
  3. No Toxicity! Do not bully, harass or purposely start arguments with other members.
  4. No NSFW! NSFW content is not allowed in the server, and will be harshly punsished. This includes sprays, text chat, and in-game voice chat.
  5. No Controvertial Topics! Avoid bringing up topics that would cause arguments between members, or would be considered offensive.
  6. No Spamming! Spamming the text or voice chats can get you muted or up to banned from the server, depending on severity.
  7. No Piracy! Discussion/ encouragement of piracy will be harshly punished, and attempting to provide methods of piracy will be met with an instantaneous and permanent ban.
  8. No Self-Promotion! Do not share link in chat, unless it is with one of our parntered servers.
  9. Keep Chat in English! In order to provide fair and accurate moderation, keep the chat in English. Conversations in other languages are allowed only when a moderator present in fluent enough to moderate properly.
  10. Use Common Sense! Just because it isn't explicitly stated in the rules, does not mean it is allowed. Looking for or using loopholes is prohibited.
  11. Respect Staff Decisions! Do not be disrespectful of staff members for moderation choices. If you disagree with a staff member's decision, be respectful and consise.
  12. If there is someone breaking a rule or you wish to appeal for a moderation decision made in the server, please open a ticket in the Kasane Teto Discord server using !!send and make sure to say "Tetocord TF2 server" somewhere for us to know it's about something here.
